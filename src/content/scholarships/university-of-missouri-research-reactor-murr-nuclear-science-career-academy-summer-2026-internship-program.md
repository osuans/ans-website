This program offers a world-class opportunity for students in nuclear science and related fields to gain **hands-on, practical experience** in isotope production and the broader nuclear industry.

---

### **📍 Program Overview**
*   **Location:** On-site at the MURR facility (Columbia, MO).
*   **Duration:** Summer 2026 semester.
*   **Target Fields:** Nuclear science, radiochemistry, physics, engineering, biology, communications, policy, finance, and accounting.

### **🔬 Technical Divisions**
Interns will be placed within one of MURR’s core operating or research divisions, including:
*   **Materials Science Program**
*   **Neutron Scattering**
*   **Archaeometry Center**

### **🎓 Key Benefits**
*   **Career Exploration:** Direct exposure to the nuclear industry’s regulatory and business environments.
*   **Micro-credentialing:** Targeted training in specialized areas such as:
    *   10 CFR 50/59 license amendment processes.
    *   Overview of **cGMP** and **FDA documentation** practices.
    *   Regulatory agency roles and functions.
*   **Networking:** Opportunities to connect with industry professionals and peers.
