Step into the future of nuclear engineering at "The Nuclear Frontier" documentary screening and discussion panel! Join us on September 17, 2025, from 4:30 PM to 7:00 PM, for a transformative evening hosted by ANS at OSU and The Nuclear Company. This is your exclusive chance to engage with leading experts from academia, industry, and the US Navy as they delve into the groundbreaking advancements and limitless possibilities of nuclear technology. We will feature panelists from The Ohio State University, Centrus Energy, and the U.S. Navy.

Sandwiches will also be served starting at 4:30 PM in the basement of Scott Laboratory E, before the meeting. This brief, informal period will provide time to socialize and network before the formal agenda begins.

Seize this opportunity to broaden your horizons, network with industry pioneers, and play a pivotal role in shaping the future of nuclear energy in the United States. Don’t just witness the evolution—be a part of it! Reserve your place now and contribute to the conversation that drives innovation and progress!
